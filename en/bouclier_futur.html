A Futuristic Shield

In a futuristic world, films suggest that physical shields can incorporate new technologies (components, artificial intelligence...) with the assembly of new materials to enhance their properties.

An Immaterial Shield

Beyond the fact that a shield can be purely physical, with the evolution of technology, cyberattacks could improve and create new types of attacks that could lead to cyber warfare. Consequently, virtual shields must be further strengthened and encourage society to secure their confidential data.

Are Values Still Present?

Regarding the symbolic aspects of the shield in the future, it is clear that certain defensive values are still present. This legacy of protection passed into the future will remain, such as justice and protection. However, this depends on the personality of the holder.

However, with the advancement of technology, immaterial shields do not possess the same values as physical shields since they are no longer tangible. Therefore, their valiant and symbolic aspects will only be digital and technical.
