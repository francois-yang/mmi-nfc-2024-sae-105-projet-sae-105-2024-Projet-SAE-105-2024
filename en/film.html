Movie Summary

Captain America: First Avenger is a 2011 movie that tells the origins of the superhero Steve Rogers, aka Captain America, during World War II. Steve Rogers is a frail and patriotic young man who dreams of joining the American army to fight against the Nazis.

The Transformation of Steve Rogers

Rejected multiple times due to his weak constitution, he is eventually chosen to participate in an experimental program aimed at creating super-soldiers. The Role of the Shield

Designed for defense, the shield quickly becomes Captain America's most iconic weapon. Steve uses it to protect himself from bullets and explosions, but also as an offensive weapon by throwing it at his enemies, after which it returns to him like a boomerang. Captain America‚Äôs shield is a powerful symbol of protection because he is, above all, a protector. His shield embodies his values: he defends the oppressed, protects his comrades, and fights for justice and freedom.
