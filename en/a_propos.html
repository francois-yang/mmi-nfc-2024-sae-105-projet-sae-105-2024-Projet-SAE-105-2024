Who am I?

Hello, my name is YANG François, and I am a first-year student in the BUT MMI (Multimedia and Internet Professions) program.

What is this about?

This website was created as part of a university project aimed at exploring a symbolic object, here the shield, through the theme "Prehistory of the Future," proposed by our professors.

Why choose this object?

I chose the shield because it is an emblematic object that has served humanity greatly. From Antiquity to the present day, shields have retained great symbolic value. I found it interesting to discuss it because it is still present in our societies today.

Glossary

  • The Pleiades (or Pleiads): A group of seven stars well known in Greek mythology and ancient astronomy.
